Dry Spell Analysis · Chandrapur, Wardha, Akola & Washim Districts · Meteorological Weeks 24–40

Weekly Dry Spell Days During Monsoon

One chart per year (2015–2025), comparing Chandrapur, Wardha, Akola and Washim. For each meteorological week, the line shows how many of that week's 7 days had rainfall (RF) below 2.5 mm — the standard threshold for a dry day. A value of 7 means the entire week was dry; a low value means the week was mostly rainy. Gaps in the Washim line reflect weeks with no recorded station data.
